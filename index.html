Ingredients

  • 1 package spaghetti
  • 1 cup mint leaves
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • Mozarella cheese to taste

Spaghetti Arrabbiatta

Description

This is a spicy spaghetti dish with fresh mint cooked in for freshness.

Directions

  1. Cook spaghetti in simmering water for 20 minutes or until al dente
  2. Strain spaghetti and put in a pan with spaghetti sauce and mint leaves
  3. Stir and add paprika as desired
  4. Grate mozarella cheese on top, to your taste
